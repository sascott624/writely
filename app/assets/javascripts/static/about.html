Welcome to Writely! A site for inspiration and creative writing.

The idea behind Writely is simple: on the create page, you are given a directive to write a short sample using a randomly generated word. These samples range from a standard dialogue or poem, to the more obscure (such as a doctor's note or tardy excuse).

If the selected word does not suit, you may simply click the word to generate a new, random selection (the same may be done for the genre of writing).

Once you are satisfied with your writing sample, you may publish it to our archive for other writers to view! You may browse through the archive, or you also have the option of allowing our random tab to randomly select a writing sample for you to enjoy.

Thanks for stopping by!
